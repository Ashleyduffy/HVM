HEART VS. MIND





OUR
MISSION

As a company, Heart vs. Mind® strives to produce high quality goods that fuse fashion and purpose. Our main focus is to shed light on the everyday battles between the Heart and the Mind; by doing so, we hope to spread Love and Logic to individuals worldwide. Although the two are often in battle, they both share an equally important role in decision making. Heart vs. Mind® is bigger than a brand, it’s a movement!






MESSAGE FROM
THE CREATOR

“When I was sixteen years old, I envisioned a battle: Heart vs. Mind®. I immediately discovered that my life’s purpose is to broaden the perspectives of the masses by sharing this epiphany. Within each and every decision one makes in their lifetime lies a battle between the Heart and the Mind. While making decisions, one must seek the ideal balance between their thoughts and feelings, in order to achieve the most successful outcome. The Heart vs. Mind® movement represents a declaration of one’s own acceptance and mastery of this internal battle. Although it differs for everyone, my key piece of advice is: Lead With Your Heart and Let Your Mind Follow!”
– Isaac Abreu
